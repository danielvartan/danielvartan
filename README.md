### Olá, I'm Daniel! 👋

I’m a R(esearcher) at the University of São Paulo ([USP](https://www.usp.br/)), specializing in [complex systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complex_system) modeling, data science, and [research software engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Research_software_engineering), with a focus on applications in the social sciences and public health. I develop computational tools in [R](https://www.r-project.org/), [NetLogo](https://www.netlogo.org/), and [Python](https://www.python.org/), including data visualizations, graphical interfaces, and [reproducible analysis pipelines](https://sustentarea.github.io/nutritional-status/). I’m the author and maintainer of open-source [R packages](https://github.com/ropensci/mctq) and [agent-based models](https://github.com/sustentarea/logoclim), and I also contribute as a developer and collaborator with the [rOpenSci Initiative](https://ropensci.org/).
